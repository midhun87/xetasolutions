Next-Generation Technology, Human-Centered Solutions

Xeta Solutions is a next-generation technology startup focused on transforming learning, assessment, and hiring through intelligent, data-driven automation. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employability by creating products that merge AI innovation with human potential.

Our flagship platform, Testify-LAC, empowers institutions and organizations to conduct AI-powered assessments, proctored exams, and interactive learning experiences. Testify goes beyond traditional testing — it measures capability, not just correctness.
